CDT: policy commentary, not a bill tracker

Important distinction: unlike every other section on this page, CDT's "AI Policy Tracker" is not a database of bills. It's the Center for Democracy & Technology's own log of its publications and activities (blog posts, comments, testimony, reports). No bill numbers, states, sponsors, or legislative status live in this data — so it isn't compared against the sections above.
